Location

A free shuttle bus for transportation between Hotel Indigo to/from various meeting locations will be provided. Breakfast, lunch and coffee breaks will be provided for all days.

Parking: If you will be driving to the meeting events, ample parking is available at Hotel Indigo, where the workshops will be held on Friday and Monday, and parking is free on the weekends in the lots around the Harn Museum, where the main meeting will take place on Saturday and Sunday.

Workshops Hotel Indigo

The workshops on Friday, January 3rd and Monday, January 6th will be held at Hotel Indigo. This is our conference partnered hotel - we encourage participants to make housing reservations here.

35020 SW 30th Lane, Gainesville, FL 32608




Main Meeting Harn Museum of Art

The main meeting will be held Saturday, January 4th and Sunday, January 5th at the University of Florida's Harn Museum of Art. The Harn museum is located next door to the FLMNH.

3259 Hull Rd, Gainesville, FL 32611




Evening Event Florida Museum of Natural History

The evening event on Saturday, January 4th will take place at the FLMNH. This event will include hors d'eouvres and a cash bar. Poster session will take place at this time. The FLMNH is located next door to the Harn Museum.

3215 Hull Rd, Gainesville, FL 32611

About SSB

The Society of Systematic Biologists advances the science of systematic biology in all its aspects of theory, principles, methodology, and practice, for both living and fossil organisms, with emphasis on areas of common interest to all systematic biologists regardless of individual specialization.
