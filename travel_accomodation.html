Getting to Gainesville

University of Florida • Gainesville, Florida

By Air

The Gainesville Regional Airport (GNV) is 15 minutes away from the University of Florida main campus and ~25 minutes to the conference hotel, Hotel Indigo (Get Directions). Visitors can take a taxi, Uber, Lyft or RTS Bus to the conference hotel. There are multiple daily flights from Atlanta (Delta), Charlotte (American), and Miami (American).

Gainesville is also two hours or less from three international airports.

• Jacksonville (JAX) - 1 hour, 45 minutes (Get Directions)

• Orlando (MCO) - 1 hour, 49 minutes (Get Directions)

• Tampa (TPA) - 2 hour, 2 minutes (Get Directions)

By Train

The nearest Amtrak station is located just 14 miles north of Gainesville in the town of Waldo. Visitors can take a bus from the Amtrak station to the RTS downtown station, where you can take a RTS Bus to the conference hotel.

By Bus

There are several bus companies with drop off and pick up locations on campus at the University of Florida. From campus you can take a taxi, Uber, Lyft or RTS Bus to the conference hotel.

• GMG Transport

• Greyhound

• Megabus

• RedCoach
